How it works

Simple, real, and free.

Built on top of The Whale Museum's community sighting network — thousands of volunteers, researchers, and ferry crews reporting from the water.

01

You sign up

Enter your phone number and zip code. We convert your zip to lat/lon coordinates.

02

Whales are spotted

Community members report sightings via The Whale Museum. Reports are verified and geotagged.

03

We calculate distance

We check if the sighting is within 5 miles of your zip — laterally along the same latitude band.

04

You get a text

Instant SMS with species, location, and direction of travel. No app needed.
